About Me

Hello! I'm Shivanshu a dedicated software developer with a deep passion for programming and technology. My fascination with the digital world began early in life, nurtured by the beautiful and tranquil valleys of Dehradun. Growing up in this serene and picturesque environment, surrounded by lush greenery, fueled my curiosity and creativity.
I have a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science, and over the years, I've developed a strong enthusiasm for programming languages and mathematical theory. Using Linux on a daily basis, I enjoy customizing my setup to enhance productivity and tailor it to my needs. Currently, I work on GenAI products, focusing on developing innovative solutions in the realm of artificial intelligence.
Outside of coding, I have a diverse range of interests. I'm an avid chess player, having competed at the national level during my school years and continued to pursue it at university. Chess has taught me strategic thinking and patience, qualities that I find invaluable in my professional life. I also enjoy playing snooker and running, activities that keep me physically active and mentally sharp. Drawing is another passion of mine, offering a creative outlet that complements my technical pursuits.
Growing up in Dehradun instilled in me a love for nature and outdoor activities. The serene environment not only nurtured my passion for drawing but also instilled a sense of peace and balance that I carry into my professional and personal life. I believe that technology should be both functional and elegant, and this philosophy guides my approach to software development. I strive to create solutions that are not only effective but also intuitive and user-friendly.
